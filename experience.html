Front-End Developer Intern

Developed a mobile application for SpotMeSolutions as a Front-End Developer!

The SpotMe Solutions Application is a mobile application that helps it's users find accessible parking in garages! Stemming from the issue of finding parking around downtown San Jose, SpotMe Solutions has developed a way to make finding more efficient and save people time in doing so! Page Title

Front End Team


I had an amazing time working with the SpotMeSolutions Team during the Fall of 2018!

We successfully built a working version of the application after 4 monthes of work. This is a big accomplishment, as many of us had little to no prior experience working with JavaScript; much less, React and redux!

Through working on the team, I learned a lot about what I'm capable of! I developed and improved my skillset, learning new things along the way as well as refining my understanding of stuff I had already known.

With that being said, the project was by no means easy to complete. The biggest challenge that the team faced was merging code and communication. Both problems intertwined with one another as different team members would be working on different things and not communicating that with the others. This resulted in the team stressing out and using time inefficiently. However, the problem was resolved as the guidelines for the project our team was building became more and more clear!

Although coding the project was challenging and working with team members was the best, it was worth it. Through working with the SpotMeSolutions team I learned lots of new things and have grown a fondness for UI/UX design through the process!
